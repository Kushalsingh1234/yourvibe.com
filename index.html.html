Books By Kushal
So you guys already know my name, but still to do the formalities I should repeat it. My name is Kushal Singh and I am a part time writer, so I started writing at the age of 14 years in lockdown. So the books you see above in the photos are the ones which I wrote in my teenage probably in 2020-2021.

The Hidden Battle for the nation is a patriotic spy thriller set up in Sweden and Russian countries.

Mark Vendor and the Crime Mytery was my first book. It's a suspense based plot on three friends in a school.

The Inspiration was my first and only poetry book. It is mainly dedicated to the rhyming poems on various social issues of societal concern.

The Moonlight Smile that Stole my Heart was my first properly love based story, where a love story between two tenants develop and strengthen through difficult circumstances.
